Who We Are

The Sierra Leone Football Agency (SLFA) is a development-focused organization committed to nurturing young football talent across Sierra Leone. We provide structured training programs, competitive platforms, and scouting exposure to empower the next generation of athletes.

Over the years, our academy has helped produce disciplined, skilled, and mentally strong players who continue to influence the national and international football scene.

Our Mission

To develop young footballers through structured education, professional coaching, and competitive match experience while promoting discipline, teamwork, and personal growth.

Our Vision

To become the leading youth football development institution in West Africa, producing world-class athletes and role models for the next generation.

Our Core Values

Discipline

We build strong character through consistency, respect, and focus.

Teamwork

We teach players to work together, communicate, and support one another.

Growth

We maintain a learning environment where improvement never stops.

Our Journey

SLFA began as a grassroots initiative to provide young athletes with better opportunities in football. With limited resources but unstoppable ambition, local coaches and community leaders laid the foundation of what has now become a national development powerhouse.

Today, we run structured youth leagues, training camps, scouting events, and educational programs that help shape complete athletes prepared for life on and off the pitch.
